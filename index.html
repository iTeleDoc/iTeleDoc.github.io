About Us

Welcome to iTeleDoc: Redefining Healthcare Access.

Where iTeleDoc combines expertise, empathy & cutting-edge technology to redefine the landscape of healthcare. We are dedicated to provide a comprehensive array of professional & compassionate remote healthcare services tailored to meet the diverse needs of individuals seeking quality care from the comfort of their homes.

At iTeleDoc, we harness the power of innovation to bring healthcare to your fingertips. Our comprehensive Telehealth solutions are designed to enhance patient care, streamline workflows & empower healthcare deliver.

We believe in a future where healthcare is accessible to all, irrespective of geographical constraints. With a vision to bridge the gap between patients & healthcare providers, leveraging the power of technology to create a seamless & efficient healthcare experience.

Our Services

Virtual Consultations & Diagnosis

Conducting medical consultations through video calls in modern approach to assess & diagnose patients' health conditions remotely.

Follow-up Appointments

Conducting follow-up appointments & routine checkups through virtual platforms, promoting continuity of care without the need for in-person visits.

Tele-Urgent Healthcare Services

Addressing urgent medical concerns through telehealth, offering timely medical advice & determining the necessity of in-person care when required.

Chronic Disease Management

Managing & monitoring chronic conditions through telehealth, allowing regular check-ins & adjustments to treatment plans.

Preventive Care Assessments

Access preventive care strategies & valuable health resources to empower you with the knowledge to proactively manage your well-being.

Diet & Nutrition Counseling

Receive tailored diet & nutrition counseling from experts, helping you make informed choices for a healthier lifestyle.

Health Education & Lifestyle Counseling

Providing guidance on health-related topics, offering healthcare education sessions & counseling on lifestyle changes for improved well-being.

Remote Patient Monitoring

Utilizing connected devices to monitor & track patients' vital signs, such as heart rate, blood pressure & glucose levels, from a distance.

Remote Imaging Consultations

Experience remote consultations, ensuring comprehensive reviews & discussions of diagnostic imaging results from the comfort of your home.

Electronic Prescription Services

Streamline your prescription needs with electronic prescription services, providing you with convenient & secure access to essential medications.
